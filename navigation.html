about us

This website was made to help people find books that they would enjoy reading, by providing in-depth reviews on books while also recommending books based on books that a user has previously read. Readers may also go to author profiles to read more on the background of a book, such as inspiration for books and similar topics.
This website was founded by the creators with the main intent of sharing their love of books. Unlike other websites, our website has a membership programme which gives access to certain features, such as author Q&A's and so on. This can be bought for only $59.99 a month, and $149.99 annually, enabling you to have exclusive access to book previews way before others.
